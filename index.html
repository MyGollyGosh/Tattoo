About

Welcome to my website! My work has a fine line, illustritative quality that is heavily influenced by the natural world and vintage imagery. Influenced by Beatrix Potter and anthropomorphism, I tattoo animals in a unique and endearing way, often giving them their own personality and home on the body. Applying techniques that uses fine lines for shading, my flora and fauna tattoos are unique and original.

Client oriented and considerate, I'm always happy to work around your ideas, answer any questions and offer support during the entire process. I specialise in custom designs delivering drawing and tattoos hat are exclusive to you.

To book please contact me through DM or email. I work Tuesday - Saturday at The Dollhouse in Brighton.

Aftercare

Proper aftercare is essential to ensure that your tattoo heals properly and looks great for years to come. Here are some guidelines to follow after getting a tattoo:

  • Keep the tattoo clean and dry for the first 24-48 hours.
  • After that, gently wash the tattoo with soap and water and apply a thin layer of ointment.
  • Avoid direct sunlight and soaking the tattoo in water for 2-3 weeks.
